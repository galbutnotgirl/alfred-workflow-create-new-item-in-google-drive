alfred-workflow-create-new-item-in-google-drive
===============================================

Alfred Workflows to Create a New Item in Google Drive. There's one for Google Accounts and one for Google Apps Accounts.

There's some customization needed for the Google Apps workflow:

Double click on the URLs on the right side and replace YOURDOMAIN with your domain (including .com or whatever it ends with).
